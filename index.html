About

TFM, also known as Flatfish Mafia, is an independent music project built around raw coastal storytelling, blue-collar humor, and gritty songs from the waterline.

The sound lives somewhere between marsh mud, late-night trouble, back-road balladry, and the kind of truth polite people usually try to bury under fresh carpet.

Music

TFM writes songs about fishing, friendship, hard living, survival, bad decisions, and the strange poetry of people who keep going anyway.
